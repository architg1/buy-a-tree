isn’t just an act of charity—it’s an act of necessity. Buy A Tree makes the process of buying (or funding) a tree very easy. We only feature organisations
where your donations will directly lead to the birth of a tree. Very soon, we will begin partnering with independent farmers to not only provide employment
opportunities to those who need it the most but also let you name your tree(s).


Planting trees has never been easier
  1. Select an organisation of your choice—there is no wrong choice.
